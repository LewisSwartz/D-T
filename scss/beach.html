Full ltinerary

Day 01: Yangon - Ngwe Saung Beach
This morning you get picked up from your hotel in Yangon to drive to Ngwe Saung (approximately 6-7 hours).

Accommodation at Eskala Hotel or similar.

Day 02: Ngwe Saung Beach
Enjoy this day at your leisure in Ngwe Saung. Besides relaxing and sunbathing at the beach, there are also options for doing boat trips or bike rides.

Accommodation at Eskala Hotel or similar.

Day 03: Ngwe Saung - Yangon
After breakfast, enjoy a few more hours of relaxation before driving back to Yangon. Upon arrival Yangon, transfer to either your hotel or to the airport.
